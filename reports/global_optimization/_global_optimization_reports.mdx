# Global Optimization Reports

#### `dead_columns`

Reports all dead columns in the workspace.
For all models in the workspace with both upstream & downstream dependencies, find columns which are unused.

Report crochet_shop.public.dead_columns
+------------------------+--------------------------------------+
| column_name            | table_ref                            |
+------------------------+--------------------------------------+
| myspace_account_name   | example.public.customer_address_book |
+------------------------+--------------------------------------+
1 row.

#### `dead_tables`

Reports all dead tables in the workspace.
For all tables in the workspace, creates a report which have no downstream dependencies

Report example.public.dead_tables
+-------------------------------------------+-----------------------+
| table_ref                                 | table_name            |
+-------------------------------------------+-----------------------+
| example.public.customer_address_book      | customer_address_book |
+-------------------------------------------+-----------------------+
1 row.
