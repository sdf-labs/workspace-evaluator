# Documentation Reports

#### `table_description_coverage`

Reports the percentage of tables that have descriptions

Report example.public.table_description_coverage
+---------------+------------------+----------------+--------------+
| tables_w_desc | tables_w_no_desc | table_desc_pct | total_tables |
+---------------+------------------+----------------+--------------+
| 7             | 9                | 77.7           | 9            |
+---------------+------------------+----------------+--------------+
1 row.

#### `undocumented_models`
Lists all models lacking a table-level description. Good documentation is key for data awareness and growth.

+-------------------------------------------+
